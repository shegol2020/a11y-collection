### **I. Global / UN Frameworks**

* **1948 UDHR (Universal Declaration of Human Rights):** First global human rights document. "All human beings born free and equal." Foundation for all later conventions.  
* **1975 UN Declaration on Rights of Disabled Persons:** Non-binding recommendation. Shifted focus toward equal rights and integration.  
* **1993 UN Standard Rules:** Bridged the gap between the 1975 Declaration and the 2006 CRPD. Strong moral commitment; used as a model for national laws.  
* **2006 CRPD (Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities):** **Paradigm Shift:** Moved from Medical/Charity model to Social/Rights model. Binding treaty.  
  * **CRPD Article 9 (Accessibility):** The "Must-Know." Accessibility is a **precondition** for independence.  
* **2013 Marrakesh Treaty (WIPO):** **Copyright Exception.** Allows creating accessible formats (braille/audio) for blind/visually impaired people without paying royalties.

### **II. European Legislation** 

* **1950 ECHR (European Convention on Human Rights):** Civil/political rights. Established the **European Court of Human Rights** (citizens can sue states).  
* **1961 European Social Charter:** Economic/Social rights. Specifically protects the right to independence and social integration for disabled people.  
* **2000 EU Charter of Fundamental Rights:** Legally binding since 2009\.   
* **2000 Employment Equality Directive:** Prohibits workplace discrimination based on disability (and other grounds) in the EU.  
* **2014 eIDAS Regulation:** Electronic ID & Trust Services. Mandates that public digital IDs must be accessible to ensure inclusive digital citizenship.  
* **2016 WAD (Web Accessibility Directive):** Focus: **Public Sector.** Requires EU public bodies to make websites/apps accessible (complies with EN 301 549).  
* **2019 EAA (European Accessibility Act):** Focus: **Private Sector.** Harmonizes rules for products/services (ATMs, e-books, e-commerce) across the EU.

### **III. European Technical Standards** 

* **2019 EN 17161:** **Process Standard.** How to integrate "Design for All" into an organization's processes/management.  
* **2021 EN 17210:** **Built Environment.** Functional requirements for accessible buildings and public spaces (replaces diverse national standards).

### **IV. North American Legislation**

* **1973 US Rehabilitation Act (Section 508):** Focus: **Federal Procurement.** Requires US federal agencies to buy/use accessible ICT. Global precedent for IT standards.  
* **1990 ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act):** **Civil Rights Law.** Prohibits discrimination in all areas of public life (jobs, schools, transportation, private places open to public).  
* **2001 ODA (Ontarians with Disabilities Act):** Early Canadian provincial law. Set the stage for the stronger 2005 AODA (Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act).

### **V. United Kingdom**

* **2010 Equality Act:** Consolidated previous UK laws (like the DDA 1995). Protects against discrimination in work/society. Key concept: Duty to make **"Reasonable Adjustments."**
